Muir Woods

Muir Woods National Monument is a protected old-growth redwood forest in Marin County, California. Just north of San Francisco, it’s famous for its towering coastal redwoods—some over 1,000 years old and reaching more than 250 feet tall. The park offers serene trails, lush greenery, and a glimpse into one of the last remaining redwood ecosystems near the Bay Area.
